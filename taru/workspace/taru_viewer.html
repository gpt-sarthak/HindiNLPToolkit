तरु — Hindi Tree & Surprisal Toolkit

Muraliedhar Kanchibhotla · Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur

Parse Hindi with the built-in HDTB model or a model you train on your own corpus.

Input Model Surprisal ⌘/Ctrl + Enter parses. Turn surprisal off for tree-only (faster).
Input
From sentences: your sentences are parsed with the base HDTB model to generate a .linetrees training set (silver trees), a fresh Berkeley grammar is trained on them, and converted into a synproc surprisal model. From dependency trees: the CoNLL-U is converted directly into gold constituency linetrees and trained the same way (higher quality, since trees are annotated, not predicted). You get the model + the generated training dataset to download, and the model becomes selectable in the Parse tab. Custom models live until the Space restarts — download to keep.
